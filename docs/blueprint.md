# **App Name**: BookEase Accommodations

## Core Features:

- Customer Calendar View: Display a monthly calendar grid to customers with tappable dates, marking unavailable dates as crossed out and disabled.
- Transient Guest Booking Form: Enable customers to fill out a booking form with duration of stay, full name, mobile number, number of guests, and names of guests.
- Payment Instructions: Display clear payment instructions after booking submission, including GCash details (number or QR code) or cash arrangement instructions.
- Owner Calendar Dashboard: Provide owners with a calendar view that shows all booking entries with color-coded statuses (pending, confirmed, cancelled).
- Booking Detail Panel: Enable owners to view and edit booking details, including customer input fields and action buttons to confirm or delete bookings.
- Payment Status Management: Allow owners to manually manage the payment status using a toggle or dropdown with options like Pending, Paid via GCash, Cash on Arrival, or Cancelled.
- Fraud Detection Tool: AI tool which monitors and flags potentially fraudulent booking patterns based on booking details and payment methods.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#2E4A62) to inspire trust and relaxation.
- Background color: Light gray (#E8E9EA) for a clean and modern look.
- Accent color: Soft gold (#B09B71) to denote prestige and quality.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font offering a neutral and machined aesthetic ideal for maintaining legibility across different screen sizes.
- Use minimalist, universally recognizable icons for calendar navigation, booking status, and payment options.
- Employ a fluid grid system to ensure the app adapts seamlessly to different mobile screen sizes, providing a consistent experience.
- Subtle transitions and loading animations to provide feedback and keep users engaged without being distracting.
